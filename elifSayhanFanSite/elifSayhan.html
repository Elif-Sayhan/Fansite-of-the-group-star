Beet Hummus & Seed Crackers

Servings: 8 servings
Prep Time: 25 min
Cook Time: 3 to 3½ hrs

This beautiful hummus has all the traditional ingredients you know and love, such as chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, but adds one special additional ingredient—purple sweet potato.

Serve it with these satisfying crunchy homemade seed crackers for a nourishing snack or appetizer everyone will love.

This recipe is loaded with omega-3 fats, fiber, and plant-based protein. Beets are packed with antioxidants, fiber, vitamin C, and anthocyanins.

Purple Hummus Ingredients

  • 1 (1-pound) red beet, baked (about 1½ - 2 cups)
  • 3 large garlic cloves, unpeeled
  • 1 cup canned low-sodium organic chickpeas
  • ⅓ cup chickpea liquid
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • ¼ cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon sea salt

Seed Crackers Ingredients

  • ½ cup raw sunflower seeds
  • ½ cup ground flaxseed
  • ½ cup whole flaxseed
  • ⅓ cup raw sesame seeds
  • ¼ cup chia seeds
  • 1½ teaspoons za'atar spice
  • ½ teaspoon whole cumin seeds
  • ½ teaspoon whole anise seeds
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1¼ cups filtered water

Optional Toppings

  • Micro cilantro
  • Radish
  • Za'atar
  • Olive oil
  • Chives
